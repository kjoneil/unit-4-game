Crystal Collector!

How to play:

1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each crystal is hidden from unt you until clicked on.

5. Each time the game starts, it will change the value of each crystal.
